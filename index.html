2020 06 16 Features Overview

16.06.2020 20:03
Good afternoon criminals. I am going to share with you some features that you will find in the prison. You can enter and leave your cell You can look yourself at the mirror You can walk around the different prison areas You can fight other inmates, winning, loosing or drawing depends on your skills! I hope you don’t start a fight with any guards for your own safety! You can watch how other inmates fight You can join a fight in any of the two sides (attackers or defenders) You will gain experience and be able to improve your attributes (like strength or endurance) Your reputation will affect how others view you
